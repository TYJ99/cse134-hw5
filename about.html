About

Hi, I am Yen-Ju Tseng. I'm currently pursuing master degree at UCSD ECE. I am looking for a full-time job in the field of SDE. I have some experience in C/C++ and java. I built an Non-compressing Archival Storage System in C++ that implement 6 major actions that the Archive system supports: add, extract, remove(permanently), list, performing a diagnostic dump of all the blocks in the file, and compact(remove empty blocks from Archive). Moreover, apply Observer Pattern to the Archive to register one or more "observers" of the Archive. Observers will be notified when the Archive performs an action(add, extract, etc.). Based on the experience of making Archive storage system, I designed and built a working database like MySQL using C++17. For more details on projects section. Happy to connect and thanks.
